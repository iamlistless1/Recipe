Fish and Chips

Ingridients:

    For fish:

  • 2 large fillets of white fish (cod, haddock, or basa)
  • Salt & pepper
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour (for dusting)

    • For batter:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp cornflour (cornstarch)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup cold beer 🍺 (lager works best)

    • For chips:

  • 3–4 large potatoes (Russet or any starchy type)
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt

Preparation:

  1. Prepare the chips:
    1. Peel and cut potatoes into thick strips
    2. Soak in cold water for 30 minutes (removes starch)
    3. Dry thoroughly
    4. Fry at low temp (~150°C) for 4–5 mins (they should be soft, not brown)
    5. Remove and rest
    6. Fry again at high temp (~180°C) until golden and crispy
      👉This double-fry method is key!
  2. Make the batter:
    • Mix flour, cornflour, baking powder, and salt
    • Slowly whisk in cold beer until smooth
      👉 Batter should be thick but slightly runny
      👉 Cold beer = crispier coating
  3. Prepare the fish:
    • Pat fish dry
    • Season with salt & pepper
    • Lightly coat with flour (helps batter stick)
  4. Fry the fish:
    1. Heat oil to 180°C
    2. Dip fish into batter
    3. Carefully place in hot oil
    4. Fry for 5–7 minutes until golden and crisp
    5. Remove and drain on paper towels
