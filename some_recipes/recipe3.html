Shepherd's Pie

Ingridients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, corn)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste

For topping:

  • 3–4 potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp milk

Preparation:

  1. Make filling
    • Heat oil → sauté onion + garlic
    • Add vegetables, cook 5–7 mins
    • Add salt, pepper, tomato sauce
      👉 Cook till slightly thick
  2. Prepare mash
    • Mash potatoes with butter + milk
      👉 Smooth and creamy
  3. Assemble & bake
    • Put veggie mix in a dish
    • Spread mashed potatoes on top
    • Bake at 180°C for 20 mins
      👉 Top turns slightly golden
